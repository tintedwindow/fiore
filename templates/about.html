About Fiore
Journaling is probably one of the most rewarding habits one can develop. And when it comes to developing a habit, behaviour change plays a big role.
As James Clear said in Atomic Habits, make it obvious, make it attractive, make it easy, make it satisfying. Fiore tries to do just that to the way we journal!
Just pick a date from your calendar, upload an image, add a description if you like, and done. Fiore supports the most common image formats and has a very minimal UI, reducing the friction between you and your next journal entry. You can reflect each day with a picture, which becomes a permanent memory in that rectangular frame, and all these frames come together to make your very own Fiore!
Fiore is inspired by a touch of Italy. The islands, the landscapes, and the beaches, they reflect the calmness that comes with intentional living and the calmness that Fiore brings with it's journaling.
We hope you enjoy your stay at Fiore!
