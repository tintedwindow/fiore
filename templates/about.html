About Fiore
Fiore combines the ideas of journaling with minimalism, giving you an experience that
reflects on your life via a visual calendar.
Fiore works by framing instances on rectangles everyday and creating a calendar that is uniquely yours. It's like flipping a page on your calendar, but on each day, you see a glimpse of what your day was like. Fiore supports the most common image formats and has a very minimal UI, reducing the friction between you and your next journal entry.
Fiore is inspired by a touch of Italy. The islands, the landscapes and the beaches, they reflect the calmness that comes with intentional living and the calmness that Fiore brings to your journaling experience.
